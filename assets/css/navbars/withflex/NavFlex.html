So...block elements normally doesnt take full height of the parent. They do take full width though. In this example, the anchors cant fill the list item (yellow area). Neither do the list items fill the ul, as expected.

Here anchors are inline-block, and don't fill horizontal space.

Nav Using Flexbox

  • li
  • with
  • Flexbox

Flex containers' children do fill the cross axis (not the main axis). To occupy 100% of main we have to set 100% of children length manually.

Here anchors are displayed as blocks, and do fill hspace.

Conclusion: I'd rather use flex to build navigation bars. Also positioning the elements is way easier.
